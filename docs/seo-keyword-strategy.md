## Content Optimization Strategy

### Homepage/Landing Page
**Primary Focus:** Brand awareness + conversion
**Target Keywords:** ai tutoring software, personalized learning platform
**Optimization Elements:**
- Compelling headline with primary keyword
- Clear value proposition above the fold
- Social proof and testimonials
- Strong call-to-action buttons
- Feature highlights with relevant keywords

### Pricing Page
**Primary Focus:** Commercial conversion
**Target Keywords:** ai tutoring pricing, educational platform costs
**Optimization Elements:**
- Clear pricing comparison table
- Value-focused feature descriptions
- Trust signals and guarantees
- Urgency and scarcity elements
- Multiple payment option messaging

### Features Page
**Primary Focus:** Education + Differentiation
**Target Keywords:** ai homework helper, educational ai tools
**Optimization Elements:**
- Detailed feature breakdowns
- Use case scenarios
- Benefit-focused copywriting
- Technical specifications
- Integration capabilities

### Blog Content Strategy
**Content Pillars:**
1. **Educational Technology Trends** - Thought leadership
2. **Learning Methodologies** - Educational value
3. **Parent Resources** - Audience-specific content
4. **Student Success Stories** - Social proof
5. **AI in Education** - Industry expertise

**Blog Post Ideas:**
- "How AI is Revolutionizing Personalized Learning"
- "The Benefits of Adaptive Learning Technology for Students"
- "Parent's Guide to Choosing the Right AI Tutoring Platform"
- "5 Ways AI Tutors Outperform Traditional Homework Help"
- "Measuring Learning Progress: Analytics That Matter"

### Internal Linking Strategy
**Hub and Spoke Model:**
- Hub Pages: Homepage, Features, Pricing
- Spoke Pages: Blog posts, Resource pages, Case studies
- Link depth: 2-3 clicks from homepage to any content

**Anchor Text Distribution:**
- 40% Branded (LanaMind, Lana AI)
- 30% Exact match keywords
- 20% Partial match keywords
- 10% Generic (click here, learn more)

## Technical Content Optimization

### URL Structure:
- Descriptive, keyword-rich slugs
- Hyphen separation (no underscores)
- Lowercase only
- Remove stop words where appropriate

### Heading Structure:
- H1: Primary keyword + value proposition
- H2: Supporting keywords + benefits
- H3: Feature-specific keywords
- H4: Detailed specifications

### Image Optimization:
- Descriptive filenames with keywords
- Comprehensive alt text (70-100 characters)
- Contextual captions
- Proper sizing and compression
